Project Overview

This independent research project aims to develop a model that can predict the function of genetic circuits based on their design. The goal is to eventually generate novel synthetic circuits by training the model on public datasets from the iGEM Registry and SynBioHub.

Long-Term Vision

The ultimate goal is to build a personalized genetic circuit design tool to support my future research in synthetic biology and wet-lab testing.
